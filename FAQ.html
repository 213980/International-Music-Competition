1. Who can take part in this competition

The Music Competition is an international competition. We welcome applications from all participants worldwide, professionals and amateurs. There are age restrictions for the various instrumental categories. Please check our page for more details and information.

2. Can I participate in multiple categories?

Yes, you can.

3. Is there live competition?

This year's competition will only be offered both online and face to face.

4. Do I need to submit video to qualify for the competition?

No, you do not need to submit any video to qualify for the competition

5. I am interested in the virtual competition, when do I submit my video?

Registration and submission of video may be done any time before 2 January 2024. Please note that the video must be recorded within 3 months of the application date (not application deadline).

6. What are the prizes for the virtual and Face to Face competition?

All participants will receive an certificate Award based on their performance submitted. 1st prize winners will be invited to perform at the Hong Kong's Y-Theatre.

7. How do I submit my performance video file? (For Virtual Competition)

We have provided you two options to submit the video file. You may provide us the link in the form through a file sharing site (in the hyperlink) and the other to upload your file directly. Please note that we do not accept any link from video streaming sites such as Youtube and etc.

8. Can I record and submit multiple videos for virtual competition?

Submitted videos must not be edited or remastered in any way. Audio and video must be synchronized without any evidence of separate recordings being edited together. If there are two works presented, each work may be recorded and uploaded separately.

9. Who are the adjudicators?

The adjudicators (Dennis Fisher, Felix Hauswirth, Tan Aik Kee, Surapol Thanyawibool, Mark Suter and Hans Zimmer)(For Band)(Jonathan Philip Moffett, Phil Collins, Ringo Starr, Bill Bruford, Roger Taylor and Sheila Cecilia Escovedo)(For Drums)from other countries will adjudicate this competition. The adjudicators will be announced prior to the start of the competition.
