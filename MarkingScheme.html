Marking Scheme

1. Each participating band will be assessed according to the following criteria based on their performance:

  • Sound: Intonation, Tone Quality, Balance
  • Technique: Articulation, Ensemble Musicianship, Rhythm
  • Musical Performance: Phrasing, Dynamics, Interpretation
  • Blend: Timbre, Blend, Contrast
  • Repertoire Choice: Suitability of work for the ensemble to showcase substantial technical abilities and musical depth.

2. The final result will be the averaged mark given by all adjudicators, rounded up to 2 decimal places, totaled at 200 marks. In the case of a draw between two or more bands, the Division winner will be decided by the adjudicators vote.

3. [For Live Competition Only] Only top 3 groups of each Division will be announced at the end of each Division Competition. The results of all other bands will be announced at the Award Ceremony on Saturday, 31 August 2024.

4. Virtual Divisions results will be announced on 31 August 2024 through our website.

5. Each participating group will receive a written appraisal from the panel of international adjudicators and a certificate based on the following:

Scoring System
Gold 82.00 - 92.99
Sliver 72.00 - 81.99
Bronze 65.00 - 70.00
Certificate Below 65.00

6. Groups that score 94.00 marks and above will be awarded Gold with Distinction.

7. Participating groups that exceed the stipulated performance time or perform below minimum time shall result in 5% deduction of the final marks. This is an administrative, non-artistic penalty and will be made known via announcements when enforced. The results after enforcement of penalty will be final and non-negotiable.

8. Participating groups that violate any rule(s) of the Competition will have their award and benefits revoked.

9. All decisions made by the adjudicators are final. No appeal will be entertained.
